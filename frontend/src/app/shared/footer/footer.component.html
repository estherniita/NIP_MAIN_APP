The internship program is a three-year long initiative which marks a new and promising dawn in the Namibian academic sphere. Key focus is to assist students that require internships to graduate and subsequently create job ready and experienced graduates. Parallel to this objective, the program will hence be assisting institutions of high learning to find internship placements for students and in the long run accelerate job creation amongst the youth.
