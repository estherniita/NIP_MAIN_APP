The internship program is a three-year long initiative which marks a new and promising dawn in the Namibian academic sphere. Key focus is to assist students that require internships to graduate and subsequently create job ready and experienced graduates. Parallel to this objective, the program will hence be assisting institutions of high learning to find internship placements for students and in the long run accelerate job creation amongst the youth.
The National Internship Program was initiated in 2019. On 17 June 2019, MTC officially launched the Namibia National Internship Program in Windhoek.
The Program will accommodate 160 interns per year that will be placed at various public and private institutions. MTC will pay an allowance of N$3000 per student over a period of 3 months, and where a student requires six months internship, an amount of N$1500 will be paid.

The education institutions participating in the MTC Namibia National Internship program are:
  1. International University of Management (IUM)
  2. Namibia University of Science and Technology (NUST)
  3. Namibian Institute of Mining and Technology (NIMT)
  4. University of Namibia (UNAM)
  5. Vocation Training Centers(VTC) through the Namibia Training Authority (NTA)
